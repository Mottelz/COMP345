Detailed Description

Territory sometimes called country in the docs.

It is the smallest area on a map. (Basically, the nodes on the map.)

Parameters
m_TerritoryNameThe name of the m_TerritoryName.
m_PositionX and Y of the territories location. THis is given with all valid map files.
m_ContinentContinent that the territory belongs to
m_AdjacentTerritoriesNamesNames of connected territories.
m_AdjacentTerritoriesPointers to connected territories.
m_TerritoryIDTerritory's ID.
m_OwnerIDOwner's ID.
m_ArmiesNumber of occupying armies.
m_OwnerPointer to the owning Player.
